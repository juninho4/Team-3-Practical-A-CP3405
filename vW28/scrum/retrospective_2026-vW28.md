## Retrospective Summary

### Retrospective Summary

This sprint highlighted the importance of completing tasks on time and maintaining clear communication. Although the team worked well together, delays in several role responsibilities affected the overall sprint progress. For the next sprint, we will set earlier internal deadlines, monitor progress through regular Discord updates, and address blockers as soon as they appear. These improvements will help the team work more efficiently and deliver sprint goals on schedule.
