## Retrospective Summary

Overall, the team collaborated effectively during this Sprint. We were able to align early on the Sprint Goal, which helped guide our discussions and decisions when selecting backlog items. This improved focus and reduced unnecessary work.

Communication within the team was consistent, and Daily Scrum updates helped maintain visibility of progress and quickly identify any minor blockers. We also improved our shared understanding of requirements through ongoing discussion during Sprint Planning.

From this Sprint, we learned the importance of transparency, early clarification of tasks, and keeping the Sprint Goal visible throughout the process. These practices helped the team stay coordinated and adapt smoothly when small uncertainties arose.
