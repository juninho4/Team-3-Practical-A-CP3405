## R6 Synthesis Summary

The overall AI view is **Bearish with High confidence**.

Unlike Week 22, there is almost no split between models this week. All four models selected **Bearish**, and all four gave **High confidence**. This makes the LLM consensus unusually strong.

The strongest common argument is that all three evidence layers agree:

1. **Almanac evidence** is bearish because June in a midterm election year has historically been weak.
2. **Macro / News evidence** is bearish because yields are rising, the dollar is stronger, and Middle East geopolitical risk remains active.
3. **Technical evidence** is bearish because SPX, NDX, and IWM all fell sharply, VIX rose, technology weakened, and defensive sectors outperformed.

The biggest uncertainty is whether the market has already priced in enough bad news. Defensive sectors are holding up, and oil prices falling could reduce some inflation pressure. However, the AI consensus still treats these as contradictions, not enough to reverse the bearish regime.
