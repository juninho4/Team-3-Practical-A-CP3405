# Sprint Goal 2026-W23

## Goal

Our goal by the end this week is to apply Scrum practices again in producing a comprehensive and evidence-based market forecast for the upcoming trading week.

During this sprint, the team will collect and document current market evidence, complete seasonal , macroeconomic, and technical analyses, evaluate predictions generated by multiple AI models, and use collective human judgement to product a final market outlook for Week 23 of this year.

After the data mining, we will compare this with the previous week analysis to see if our prediction holds true or not, and make some reasoning on how we can improve it, as well as identifying the problem in the prediction.
