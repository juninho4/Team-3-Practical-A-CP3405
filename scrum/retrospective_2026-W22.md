# Retrospective 2026-W22

## Role

R2 Scrum Master

## Purpose

This file records what went well, what did not go well, and what the team will improve next sprint.

---

## What Went Well

The best part of this week was teamwork and mutual support.

Even though the team was not fully organised at the beginning, members helped each other when someone was absent or delayed. Some members temporarily completed missing parts so the project could continue, then replaced those files when the correct member finished their work.

The team also communicated through Discord and each role eventually completed their assigned task.

---

## What Did Not Go Well

The biggest issue was that the team did not organise early enough.

Because this was our first week, many members were not completely sure what their role needed to do. As a result, the team did not start properly until Sunday, which created time pressure.

Some files were also not in the correct format or folder at first. For example, some documents needed to be converted into `.md` format and placed in the correct folders.

---

## Blockers

- The team did not fully understand the task structure at the start.
- Some members were unsure about their role responsibilities.
- One member was not available at first, so others had to help complete the work temporarily.
- Work started too late, mainly on Sunday.
- GitHub file organisation needed improvement.

---

## What We Learned

We learned that evidence collection alone is not enough. The team also needs clear process documentation, including standup notes, sprint goals, acceptance criteria, and retrospective records.

We also learned that starting late makes the task more stressful, even if the final content is good.

---

## Improvements for Next Sprint

Next week, the team will start earlier instead of waiting until Sunday.

The team will:

- Confirm each role at the start of the week.
- Make sure everyone understands their responsibility.
- Create Scrum process files earlier.
- Check file format and folder location before submission.
- Communicate progress earlier through Discord.
- Ask for help sooner if someone is blocked.

## Retrospective Summary

This week showed that the team can support each other, but we need better organisation and earlier communication. The main improvement for next sprint is to start earlier, confirm responsibilities sooner, and keep Scrum process documents updated throughout the week.
