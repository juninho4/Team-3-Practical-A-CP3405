## EMA Setup

The charts use two exponential moving averages:

- **EMA (8):** short-term trend indicator
- **EMA (21):** medium short-term trend indicator

When the price is above both EMA (8) and EMA (21), it suggests bullish momentum. When EMA (8) is above EMA (21), it also supports a positive short-term trend.

## Overall EMA Summary

SPY, NDX, and IWM are all trading above both the EMA (8) and EMA (21). In all three charts, the EMA (8) is also above the EMA (21), which shows positive short-term momentum.

This supports a bullish technical bias across the three major market indexes. NDX appears to have the strongest momentum because the price is furthest above both EMA lines. SPY is also bullish, while IWM remains bullish but slightly weaker because it shows a small daily pullback near resistance.
