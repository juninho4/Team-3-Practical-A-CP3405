# Sprint Goal 2026-W22

## Goal

Our goal by the end this week is to produce a reliable and evidence-based market prediction for SPX, NDX, and IWM for the upcoming trading week by combining seasonal, macroeconomic, technical, and AI-assisted analysis.

In addition, we will make sure the team follows the Scrum principles in the development of the weekly market forecasting workflow by coordinating role-based analysis, maintaining transparent documentation, and iterative feedback with each other to improve prediction quality.
