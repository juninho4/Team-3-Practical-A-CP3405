# Acceptance Criteria

The criteria should cover both market prediction and Scrum practices.

1. Market data collection is completed
Weekly performance data for SPX, NDX, IWM, Gold, Oil, 10-Year Yield, Bonds, VIX, Bitcoin, and all 11 market sectors from Yahoo Finance has been mined and recorded, every captured evidence screenshots are stored in Github.

2. All required agent analyses are completed and documented
All outputs from Almanac (historical/seasonal tendencies), Macro/News (Economic and news factors), and Technical Agent(Price action and chart analysis) have been written and commited to Github.

3. Four AI models from the three agent outputs are queried.
The same synthesis prompt is used across all four AI models (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and DeepSeek). Their raw responses have been collected from shared prompt of the three outputs. Multi-LLM comparison table is completed, identifying areas of agreement and disagreement among the models. The responses and tables are added to the repository once again.

4. Succesfully conducting a Human Score review.
Come up with the best AI recommendation and documentating any overrides or adjustments based on team judgment.

5. Ending up final predictions produced for SPX, NDX, and IWM.
Each stock are supplied with direction (Up, Down, or Flat), expected percentage range, and confidence level.

6. Team communication and progress though Scrum practices, including stand-up updates and role-based task tracking.

7. All sprint artifacts are commited to Github and organized properly.
