## What did you choose to automate and why?
We decided to go with the data mining process, which is the first step.

**It was the most repetitive manual task.** Last week, one team member had to open multiple websites, copy values, take screenshots, and commit the evidence to Github.

**It provides immediate value.** Automating data collection reduces manual workload, speeds up preparation, and gives the team a consistent starting point for analysis.

**It is realistic to build in five days** A reliable data pipeline and report generator are achievable within a short sprint, while more advanced features such as autonomous forecasting or machine learning would require more time and testing.

**It creates a foundation for future automation** Once the data pipelne is stable, we can later add automated scoring, LLM prompting, prediction aggregation, and performance trackinig without redesigning the whole system.

In the end, if we manage to finish the automation system, it should reduce the amount of manual weekend work, improve consistency across reports, and make the weekly prediction process faster and more repeatable.
