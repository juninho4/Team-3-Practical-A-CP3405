Summary

2026 is a Midterm Election Year.

According to the Stock Trader's Almanac,
Midterm Years tend to experience weakness
during Q2 and Q3.

The Almanac describes Q2-Q3 as the
'Weak Spot' of the 4-Year Cycle.

Average losses during this period are:

Dow: -2.0%
S&P 500: -2.5%
NASDAQ: -6.6%

The outlook improves significantly in Q4,
where the 'Sweet Spot' of the cycle begins.

Cautious

Bearish
